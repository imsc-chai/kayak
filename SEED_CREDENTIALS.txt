ğŸ’¾ BACKUP SOLUTION:
  To backup your database:
    1. Run: .\backend\backup-db.ps1
    2. Backups will be saved to: E:\Kayak\backups\
    3. Each backup is timestamped

  To restore from backup:
    1. Run: .\backend\restore-db.ps1 -BackupPath "E:\Kayak\backups\mongodb_backup_YYYYMMDD_HHMMSS"
    2. Replace the timestamp with your actual backup folder name
